How It Works

Most predictions rely on gut feeling. ScorePulse relies on Cold. Hard. Data.

  • Historical Regression: We analyze over 5,000 past matches to identify scoring patterns.
  • Form Correction: A weighted algorithm adjusts specifically for the last 5 games played.
  • Venue Bias: We calculate the specific "Home Advantage" coefficient for every stadium in the league.

The result is a probabilistic forecast that cuts through the noise of media hype.
